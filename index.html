Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common queries from our customers. We're here to help you make the perfect choice for your project.

Do you provide polishing and custom cutting services?

Absolutely. We offer polishing, edge finishing, and custom cutting as per your requirements to ensure the stone fits perfectly in your project.

What types of granite and marble do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of both local and imported granite and marble, including exotic quartzite. Visit our collections page or showroom to see the full range.

Do you sell only in bulk, or can I buy small quantities too?

We cater to all project sizes, from large-scale architectural projects to individual home renovations. You can purchase single slabs or in bulk.

Why should I choose Ratan Marble over others?

With over 28 years of experience, a vast collection of premium stones, and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we provide unmatched quality and service.

Are your prices competitive?

Yes, we pride ourselves on offering competitive pricing for premium quality stones by sourcing directly from quarries around the world.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location and the specifics of your order. We strive to provide timely and safe delivery for all our clients.
